About our school

Newton Institute of Technology(NIT) was founded in the year 2023 in Migori, Kenya along Migori-Sirare highway, as a modern technical college committed to excellence in vocational education. Since its establishment, NIT has specialized in high-demand technical fields such as ICT, plant operation, driving, music, and beauty therapy. Accredited by TVETA, the institute delivers hands-on, industry-aligned training aimed at producing competent and safe professionals. Over the years, it has grown its team of certified trainers and invested in state‑of‑the‑art facilities, all while maintaining a focus on safety, innovation, and lifelong learning. Under a stable and supportive leadership, NIT continues to empower its students with not only technical skills but also entrepreneurial mindsets.The institution proudly partners with global certification bodies such as Cisco Networking Academy, CompTIA, and Microsoft Learn, empowering students with industry-relevant knowledge and hands-on experience.

Vision

To be recognized as a premier technical institution that sets the standard for excellence in training, producing skilled professionals who lead with safety, competence, and innovation.

Mission

To provide a comprehensive and transformative training, empowering individuals with the skills, knowledge and confidence that they need to excel in their careers. We are dedicated to fostering a culture of continuous learning, safety-awareness, and professional growth.
